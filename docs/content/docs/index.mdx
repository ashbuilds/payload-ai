# Welcome to Payload AI

Welcome to the **Payload AI Plugin**! If you're looking to supercharge your CMS with the absolute latest in AI technology natively baked into your workflows, you've come to the perfect place. 

Whether you want to magically generate entire rich-text articles, translate your content into 40+ languages with a click, or summon perfectly proportioned images right into your media gallery—this plugin has your back.

> **Note:** This represents the beta branch with huge architectural upgrades. Expect blazing-fast performance and an entirely new generative Lexical experience!

## What can it do?

Payload AI is not just a wrapper for a single API. It is an infrastructure layer built on top of the Vercel AI SDK, meaning you have access to virtually every major AI model on the planet (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini, xAI, DeepSeek, and more) inside Payload.

Here are the headline features:
- **🪄 Lexical Compose:** A magic button attached to your Rich Text and Text fields that generates content directly into the editor.
- **✨ Text Actions:** Select any text in Lexical to instantly Rephrase, Summarize, Proofread, or change the Tone.
- **🌍 Instant Translation:** Select text and seamlessly translate it while preserving all your complex Payload Lexical formatting.
- **🎨 Image Generation:** Hook into Fal.ai, Google, or OpenAI to generate breathtaking images and save them straight to your Payload Upload collections.
- **🧱 Context-Aware Mentions:** Use the `#` syntax in your prompts to inject real-time data from other fields on your current page right into the AI's "brain". 
- **⚙️ The `payload.ai` API:** A gorgeous backend abstraction so you can use `payload.ai.generateText()` safely inside your own custom endpoints.

## Why is it so cool?

1. **Zero Vendor Lock-in:** Tired of OpenAI? Switch to Anthropic or Gemini globally from the Payload Admin UI in literally 3 seconds. 
2. **BYOK (Bring Your Own Key):** All API keys are securely managed within your database globals, not hardcoded into your environment unless you want them to be.
3. **OpenAI Compatible:** Want to use DeepSeek or Groq? We natively support custom OpenAI-compatible endpoints.
